ABOUT ME

I’ve always been fascinated by incidences where form and function meet in ways that are beautiful. These intersections occur in nearly everything in the natural world, which is why I chose to study biology in college. I find one of the most amazing intersections of form and function in the human brain—particularly the frontal lobe. It’s what differentiates humans from every other species and is the seat of our unique behavior. I studied the frontal lobe and human behavior while working in a neuroscience research lab for two years. However, as a lifelong creative, I found myself craving more artistic outlets. So I started diving into making art and music outside of my job. Now, as a UX/UI designer, I consider the relationship between human behavior and technology. I want to design an elegant form meet your function.
